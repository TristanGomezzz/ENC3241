# Instructions

How to Change Oil in a Car

Steps

Step 1- Warm up the engine.

Start the car and let it run for 2-3 minutes, turn it off after.

Step 2- Lift the car safely

Park on a flat surface, engage the parking brake and use ramps or a jack

Step 3- Locate the oil drain plug

<img width="422" height="282" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f1b97497-bec1-47ce-ba93-284e2173d61b" />
 
Figure 1 location of the oil drain plug. Source: https://www.vanagon.org/oil-change-oil-filter

Step 4- Drain the old oil.

Place drain pan underneath, then use a socket wrench to loosen the plug and remove it by hand and let oil drain.

Step 5- Reinstall the drain plug.

Screw it back by hand and tighten with the wrench.

Step 6- Remove the old oil filter.

Locate the oil filter and use the oil filter wrench to loosen it.

<img width="274" height="182" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/062ae150-995b-4c24-b6d1-05512b6da8e4" />

Figure 2 Oil filter. Source: https://www.autostop.com.au/auto-stop-news/how-often-should-you-change-your-cars-oil-filter/

Open the hood and remove the oil cap. Then use a funnel to pour it.
